The Vision

For those who couldn't have children, we offer a way to explore what could have been. A multi-sensory simulation from birth to 18 years, where the most advanced technology meets the deepest emotionality.

Living Legacy is not just a simulation; it's a bridge to understanding parenthood, human connection, and the meaning of leaving a mark on a life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Living Legacy exactly?

Living Legacy is a conceptual project that aims to create an immersive simulation of parenthood for those who could not have children. It uses a combination of XR, emotional AI, haptics, and BCI to create a deeply personal and emotional journey.

Is this a real product?

Currently, Living Legacy is a conceptual vision. The technologies involved are on the cutting edge, and this project serves as a north star for what could be possible in the future of human-computer interaction and emotional simulation.
